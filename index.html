Hi, my name is AJ!

A full-stack software developer

Growing up, I was always fascinated by programming. I was first introduced to programming through Lego Mindstorms. I loved that you could create real world impacts through a virtual piece of code. After completing my mechanical engineering, I worked in the Natural Resources industry and found myself often being frustrated with the lack of automation for relatively easy and menial business processes. So I taught myself programming and am currently leading a project to automate maintenance of industrial equipment by using IoT sensors and data analytics. I am passionate about automating processes to improve efficiency and meet business needs.
